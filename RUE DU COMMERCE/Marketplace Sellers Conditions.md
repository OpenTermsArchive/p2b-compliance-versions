La société RUE DU COMMERCE exploite un site Internet de vente en ligne sur lequel elle exerce une double activité

Partenaires Marchands.

qualité offrant toute garantie au client final sur la prestation délivrée.



Le Partenaire Marchand souhaite bénéficier d’un espace de vente au sein de la Galerie Marchande pour

et process mis en place.

dépendante de la qualité des offres proposées et de l’investissement des Partenaires Marchands à la satisfaction

exécution des obligations résultant des présentes Conditions Générales.

Marchands de la Galerie marchande, sa fiche produit regroupera plusieurs offres.

Le classement de ces différentes offres, et notamment la détermination de l’offre qui apparaîtra à titre principal

sur la fiche produit, tient compte de plusieurs éléments :



\- Le prix du produit ;

\- Les frais de livraison ;

\- Les délais de livraison.



RUE DU COMMERCE va opérer une agrégation pondérée de ces différents éléments.

- choisir « autre » si le transporteur n’est pas dans la liste et confirmer : les coordonnées complètes du

Article L.211-4

The seller must deliver an item in conformity with the contract and respond to any conformity faults existing at

the time of delivery.

They must also address the lack of conformity resulting from the packaging, instructions for assembly or

installation for which it is responsible under the contract or was carried out under their responsibility.



Article L.211-5

To comply with the contract, the product must:

1° be fit for the purpose usually expected of a similar product and, where applicable:

\- correspond to the description given by the seller and possess the features they presented to the buyer as a

sample or model;

\- present the features that a buyer might reasonably expect given public statements made by the seller, by the

producer or their representative, particularly in terms of advertising or labelling;

2° or present the characteristics defined by mutual agreement by the parties or be suitable for any specific

purpose sought by the buyer, which was made known to the seller and which the latter agreed to.



Article L.211-12

Action arising from lack of conformity is limited to two years from the delivery of the product.

Article 1641

The seller guarantees the item sold against latent defects which render it unfit for the use for which it was

intended, or which impair the use to such an extent that the buyer would not have acquired it, or would only

have given a lesser price for it, had he known of them.



Article 1648

Action arising from latent defects prohibitive to the sale must be brought by the purchaser within two years

following discovery of the defect.
